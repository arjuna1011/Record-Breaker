Record Breaker

Welcome to Record Breaker, a cookie clicker like game! You are an up and coming artist and want to make it to the top of the charts for record sales, in other words you want to break records. As you progress through your fame, you will have to upgrade your strategies to attract new listeners. This can vary from a local record store selling your records, to your own deal, to even a SuperBowl performance. Do you have what it takes to make it big? Find out here.
